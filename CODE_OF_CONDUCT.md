# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for anyone who wants to help make it better — regardless of experience level, background, identity, or skill.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that help:

- Assuming good faith in reviews, issues, and comments.
- Being specific about what isn't working, not about who broke it.
- Accepting that not every idea lands and that "no" or "not now" isn't personal.
- Using inclusive language.

Examples of behavior that don't belong here:

- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained sarcasm.
- Harassment in any form — public or private.
- Sharing someone's private information without their consent.
- Any conduct a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, commit messages, and any off-platform communication where you're representing the project.

## Enforcement

Email **antoine@chessatlas.net** for anything that falls outside the standards above. Reports are handled confidentially.

Consequences scale with the behavior: a private clarification, a public correction, a temporary interaction ban, or a permanent ban.
